Our Beginning

Built in Chicago, Driven by Innovation

Mid City Engineering was founded in 2000 with a clear vision: to create automotive electronics that integrate so seamlessly, they could be mistaken for factory-installed. From a small workshop on Chicago's West Side, we set out to solve a problem no one else was addressing — bringing truly premium, OEM-quality accessories to luxury vehicles.

Our breakthrough came with the SmartKey Starter for Mercedes-Benz, the first plug-and-play remote start solution engineered specifically for luxury vehicles. Unlike aftermarket products that required cutting wires and voiding warranties, our approach preserved the vehicle's factory integrity while adding capabilities the manufacturer never offered.

That philosophy — engineering products that belong — has guided every decision since. Whether it's our Escort Radar cluster integration that displays alerts through your factory instrument panel, or our motorized license plate systems, every product is designed to feel like it came with the car.

Today, all design, prototyping, and manufacturing still happens at our Chicago headquarters. Every unit is individually tested before it leaves our facility, because our reputation depends on the same precision our customers expect from their vehicles.
