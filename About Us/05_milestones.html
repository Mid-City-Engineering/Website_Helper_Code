Our Journey

Key Milestones

2005
Founded in Chicago
Mid City Engineering opens its doors on Chicago's West Side, beginning with custom automotive electronics for luxury vehicle owners.
2006
SmartKey Starter Launch
Introduction of the first plug-and-play remote start for Mercedes-Benz — a product that would define the company and set the standard for OEM integration.
2010
Escort Radar Cluster Integration
Pioneering the world's first seamless radar-to-instrument-cluster interface, displaying alerts through factory screens with zero visible components.
2015
Sprinter Fleet Solutions
Expansion into the commercial vehicle market with a full suite of Sprinter products, serving fleet operators, RV builders, and commercial customers.
2018
Global Expansion
Products and partnerships now span North America, India, and China — with factory-trained dealer networks providing expert installation worldwide.
Today
Continuing Innovation
From motorized license plates to 360° camera systems and alarm integration kits, MCE continues to expand its product line while staying true to its founding principle: the best, made better.
