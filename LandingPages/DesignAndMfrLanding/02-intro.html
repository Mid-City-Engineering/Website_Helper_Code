From Case Study to Global Pipeline

In 2005, we set out to control the entire design-to-manufacturing process for an automotive electronic product — domestically. Building design teams, supply chain, and manufacturing both in-house and with partners required years of relentless iteration.

Twenty years later, we have continuously deployed this process, successfully designing, manufacturing, and distributing our portfolio of products globally. Our supply chain and manufacturing now extend across the world, allowing us to offer solutions where low cost and high volume are design requirements.

Mid City Engineering can help you with product design from the conceptual stage through to manufacturing — including software, technical documents, technical support, quality control, and compliance. We can offer a domestic pipeline if this is a requirement.
