On Demand

Hide your license plate on demand

Display your plate when you want to, hide it when you don't.

Key Fob Integration

Factory keyfob control

Our custom plate controller seamlessly integrates with your vehicle. When you press lock on your keyfob the plate will display in the up position. When you press unlock on your keyfob the plate will retract in the down position.

Driver Control

Steering wheel button control

The plate can also be controlled using your factory steering wheel or turn signal controls. Adjust your plate up or down from the driver seat.
