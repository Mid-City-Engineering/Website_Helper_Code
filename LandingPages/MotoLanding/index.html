Hide your license plate on demand

Display your plate when you want to, hide it when you don't.

Factory keyfob control

Our custom plate controller seemlessly integrates with vehicle. When you press lock on your keyfob the plate will display in the up position. When you press unlock on your keyfob the plate will retract in the down position.

Steering wheel button control

The plate can also be controlled using your factory steering wheel or turn signal controls. Adjust you plate up or down from the driver seat.

Everything included

We provide everything that you need to install and control your plate. No additional parts are needed.
