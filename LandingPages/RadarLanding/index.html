Displays on your instrument cluster

Our radar interfaces integrate directly into your instrument cluster display. All radar information is displayed on OEM instrument cluster across multiple menus including alerts and options settings.

Control settings with the steering wheel buttons

Use your steering wheel buttons to change volume level, mute alerts, select option settings and enable or disable laser jamming.

Simple wire connection

Our interface quickly installs using a simple 4 wire interface. Connects to GND, 12V, and the CANBUS data lines in your vehicle. Installs in under 30 minutes.

Works with escort radar detectors

Our interface works with Escort Max, Max360, MaxCi, and Redline portable and installed radar detectors.
