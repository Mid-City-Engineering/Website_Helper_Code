Note: stand-alone high idle kits are only compatible with diesel models. For gasoline Sprinters, use our SmartKey Starter® remote start with high idle.

What is PSM? PSM is an optional upfitter module that some 906 & 907 Sprinter vans are equipped with. Consult your vehicle build sheet and/or relevant upfitters to determine if the vehicle is equipped with PSM. If the PSM is not being used for any application, it can be easily removed from the vehicle. PSM modules are plugged in underneath the driver seat.
