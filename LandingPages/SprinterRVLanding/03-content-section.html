High Idle Control

Smart Idle

Smartidle is our innovative high idle controller for your Diesel Sprinter. Smartidle allows you to set an elevated idle speed for your engine using the vehicle control stalk or an external trigger. Smartidle can be used to charge your coach batteries quickly reducing engine wear at low idle speeds and engine idle time.
