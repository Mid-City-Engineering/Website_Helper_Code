The Frustration

🔄

Always Resets

Every time you start your Porsche, it defaults back to Normal mode. Your custom drive settings? Gone.

🎵

Missing the Roar

You want to hear that exhaust note on startup, but Eco mode keeps it muted. The full symphony of your engine stays hidden.

⚙️

Constant Adjustments

Every startup means reconfiguring your suspension, throttle response, and shifting patterns. Your perfect setup shouldn't require a ritual.

⚙️

Auto Start/Stop Annoyance

Hate the engine cutting out at every stop? Normal mode forces it on. You're stuck manually switching modes every single drive just to disable it.
